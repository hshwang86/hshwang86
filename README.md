## 🔭 What I'm Building

- 🚌 **MobilHi ERP** — Integrated ERP for city bus operations (dispatch, payroll, incentive contracts, compliance), serving multiple transit companies. Django 5 · DRF · PostgreSQL · React.
- 📱 **Field Mobile Apps** — Flutter apps for driver alcohol-test compliance and on-site operations, connected to ERP backends via REST + JWT and Firebase push.
- 🤖 **AI Automation** — FastAPI + Claude/OpenAI agent pipelines for business video production and ERP customer-support automation.
- 📈 **Algorithmic Trading** — Crypto trading bots and market screeners on Upbit/Binance with ccxt, pandas-ta, and multi-timeframe analysis.
- 🎮 **Games** — Server-authoritative real-time online games and web-based economy simulation games.
